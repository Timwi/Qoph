Battleships

Battleships come in the following sizes. They may not touch each other, not even diagonally.

The numbers outside the grid show the number of battleship pieces in each row or column.

Pitfalls

Pitfalls come in the following shapes. They may be rotated but not mirrored. They may touch each other only diagonally, not orthogonally.

The area not occupied by the pitfalls must remain a single contiguous area. All pitfalls must be placed.

A pitfall may not occupy the same square as a white circle.
