Quantum Online Puzzle Hunt

Welcome to Quantum Online Puzzle Hunt, a hunt for puzzle enthusiasts of all levels. Anyone can play! Join now! — You can also join our Discord server.

What is this?

This is a puzzle hunt — an online event involving a collection of puzzles that are unlocked progressively as puzzles are solved. Teams will be ranked on a leaderboard according to their progress within 10 days from the start of the hunt. For details, see info.

When?

The hunt begins on Friday, 15 April 2022, 17:00 (UTC). Teams can solve puzzles indefinitely but the leaderboard is frozen on Monday, 25 April 2022, 17:00 (UTC).
